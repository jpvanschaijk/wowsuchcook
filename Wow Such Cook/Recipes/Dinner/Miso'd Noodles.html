Miso'd Noodles

A creamy, comforting pasta you can make in under 30 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 Serving of your choice of noodles, our favorite are egg noodles made with wheat
  • 10gr of Butter
  • 1 tbsp of Shiro Miso
  • Black pepper to taste
  • Water
  • Optional: fried shallots and garlic chives

Method

  1. Melt Butter and Miso in a pot
  2. Whisk until the mixture resembles wet sand
  3. Add your noodles and add enough water to just cover the noodles
  4. Cook untill all dente, if using wheat noodles the starches will thicken the sauce by itself, the result should be creamy and smooth
  5. Plate and Garnish with fried shallots or Crispy Chilli oil and some finely chopped garlic chives.
