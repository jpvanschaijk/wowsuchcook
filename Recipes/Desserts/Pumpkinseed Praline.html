Pumpkinseed Praline

A deepyly savory and sweet treat to add to ice cream, desserts like a chocolate mousse, or as a topping for your porridge!

Ingredients for 1 300ml jar

  • 200gr hulled pumpkinseeds
  • 200gr white sugar
  • Salt to taste

Method 1

  1. Roast the pumpkinseeds in the oven at 160C for 10-20 minutes depeniding on your desired roasty flavor.
  2. Caramelize your sugar in a pan to atleast a light amber color. Darker caramel will create more bitter and more complex caramelly flavors.
  3. Remove the pumpkinseeds from the oven and whilest you are making the caramel, after finishing the caramel immediately pour over your seeds and let it cool down to roomtemperature.
  4. Depending on what equipment you have grind it down to a paste slowly in a food proccesor. If you have the luxury, you can use a wetstone mill that the chocolatiers use to create the smoothest chocolate available.
  5. Jar it and enjoy!

Method 2

  1. Roast the pumpkinseeds in the oven at 160C for 10-20 minutes depeniding on your desired roasty flavor.
  2. Allow the seeds to cool down a bit before grinding them to a paste together with an equal amount in weight of Moscovado sugar.
  3. Jar it and enjoy!
