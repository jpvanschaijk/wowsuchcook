Dutch Pancakes

The classic Dutch pancakes!

Ingredients

  • 500ml milk
  • 3 Eggs
  • 300gr AP flour
  • 1tsp salt
  • Toppings as desired (we like bacon!)
  • Butter

Method

  1. Whisk all of the ingredients together so that there are no clumpls left. Allow to sit for an hour or even overnight.
  2. Melt butter in a hot pan (high or medium/high).
  3. Add a ladle of batter into the hot pan and swirl the pan to create an even and thin layer of pancake batter.
  4. Allow the batter to set completely and allow the bottom to brown a bit before flipping and finish baking.
