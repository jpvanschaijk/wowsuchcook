Vietnamese Pork Sauce

Savory, sweet and tangy. This glaze for you pork belly skewers will be your favorite.

Ingredients

  • 2kg brown sugar
  • 640ml soy sauce
  • 640ml rice vinegar
  • 1 bottle fish/squid sauce
  • 380ml angostura bitters
  • 240gr ginger paste
  • 240gr garlic paste
  • 120gr sirracha
  • 4tbsp salt

Method

  1. Put everything into a pot and bring to a boil, boil for approximately 15 minutes.
  2. Cool and store in a suitable container and refrigerate or freeze.
