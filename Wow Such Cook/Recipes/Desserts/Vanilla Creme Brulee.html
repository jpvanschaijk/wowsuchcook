Vanilla Creme Burlee

A creamy, comforting pasta you can make in under 30 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 5 Egg yolks
  • 1 Vanilla bean seed pod
  • 90gr White Sugar
  • 600gr Heavy Cream
  • 3gr Salt

Method

  1. Deseed the vanilla pod and put this in a small sauce pan with the creme and heat it gently until it starts steaming.
  2. Meanwhile, mix the sugar, salt and egg yolks in a bowl and whisk it untill pale and you can't see/feel any sugar crystals.
  3. Once the cream is hot, slowly temper the egg yolk mixutre by slowly whisking in a steady stream of the cream mixture, after this transfer the custard to the serving containers.
  4. Cook in a sousvide cooker for 1 hour at between 80-85C. The lower the temperature the softer and creamier the custard, the higher the temperature the more firm.
  5. After cooking for 1 hour, remove form the cooker and allow to cool to roomtemperature before chilling in the fridge for atleast 4 hours, preferably overnight.
  6. When serving, sprinkle a about a teaspoon's worth of white sugar on top of the custard before caramelizing it gently yet quickly with a torch. Allow the caramel to set before enjoying.
