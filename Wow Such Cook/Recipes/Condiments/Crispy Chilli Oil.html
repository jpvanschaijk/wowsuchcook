Crispy Chilli Oil

The best chilli oil you will ever have and make.

Ingredients

  • 70-75gr of partially deseeded chillies depending on how spicy you want your oil
  • 2-3 pieces of star anise
  • 2 pods of black cardamom, crushed
  • 25gr of ground Sichuan peppercorns
  • 20gr white sugar
  • 30gr Sea salt
  • 4-5gr MSG
  • 6gr ground cumin
  • 2gr ground black pepepr
  • 500gr neutral oil like Canola oil
  • 200gr thinly sliced shallots
  • 70gr thinly sliced garlic

Method

  1. Deseed your chillies, it's fine to leave some in depending on how spicy you like it
  2. Coursely grind you chillies in a food processor
  3. Mix all of your dry ingredients in a heat proof bowl or pan
  4. Fry your shallots until lightly browned and fragrant in the oil. Be careful to not leave them in too long as they will continue to brown and will become bitter if they are fried too long
  5. Do the same with the garlic, these need to be slightly ligher in color and will turn bitter quicker than the shallots if fried too long.
  6. Once your aromatics have been fried, heat the oil to 190 celcius and then dump over your dry ingedients and stir vigorously.
  7. Allow to cool to room temperature before removing the star anise and black cardamom pods.
  8. Ladle into your prefered containers and enjoy!
