Vanilla Custard Icecream

The best homemade ice cream you may ever have. Use this recipe as a base and swap in flavorings as you wish. Be creative!

Ingredients

  • Whole Milk
  • Cream
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla
  • Salt

Method

  1. Boil pasta until al dente
  2. Whisk eggs and cheese
  3. Combine gently off heat
